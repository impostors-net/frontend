Have you ever wondered if there’s an online version of Impostor?
If so, you’ve probably noticed that
there aren’t many true online versions, just some cheap Knock-offs.
That’s why we decided to create our own , fully online version of IMPOSTOR! How the Game Works:
In Impostor, you can make Posts and also comment under them,
In each Post, Users are either Innocents or The Impostor.
All Innocents can see the initial Post and comment under it aswell.
The Impostor cannot see the Post but only the comments
and has to blend in by making a comment based on the other players’ comments.
Players can upvote or downvote each other's comments.
After 7 day round ends and the Impostor is revealed.
You will get Rewards according to your up and down Votes
if you Downvote the comment of an Impostor as an Innocent you will gain Points, Badges and Rewards
if you get Upvotes as an Impostor you gain Points, Badges and Rewards
